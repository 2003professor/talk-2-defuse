How to Play

Communicate. Coordinate. Defuse.

One player sees the bomb. The other has the manual. Neither has both. Talk through the chat to defuse it before time runs out.

The Executor

Sees the bomb with all its modules — wires, buttons, keypads, lights. Describes everything and performs actions.

Sees Bomb
CHAT

The Instructor

Has the bomb defusal manual with rules for every module. Reads the manual and gives step-by-step instructions.

Has Manual

Game Flow

1
Describe Executor tells the Instructor what they see — bomb shape, color, serial number, module types.
2
Identify Instructor looks up the bomb in the manual's Index tab to find its defusal protocol.
3
Instruct Instructor reads the rules and tells the Executor exactly what to do for each module.
4
Execute Executor clicks components to interact — cut wires, press buttons, enter codes. A confirmation popup verifies each action.
Pro tip: The Executor should start by reading the serial number, bomb shape, and number of batteries — the Instructor needs these to find the right page in the manual.

Bomb Modules

Wires Cut the right ones

Password Spell the word

Strikes — Wrong actions add strikes. Too many and the bomb explodes!
Timer — Turns red when time is low. Strikes may speed up the countdown!
Communication is key — Be specific. Use labels, colors, positions. Don't assume!
